How do I start?

Download our app, answer 3 questions, charge your customers.

What's the cost?

3.5% + $0.35 per transaction. For example, if your customer pays you $50, your recieve $47.90 and you pay us $2.10 ($50 x 3.5% + $0.35).

How much must I pay for setup or per month?

$0. We don't charge setup or monthly fees.

How do I get my money?

You give us your banking information and your identification to verify your identity. We do payouts every Friday to your bank account. Your money arrives to your bank account in up to 2 business days. For more details you can access our terms and conditions here.
